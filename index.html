Payment Instructions

HOW TO PAY

M

MTN Mobile Money

Pay using MTN MoMo merchant code

  1. 1Dial *165*3# on your phone
  2. 2Select Option 1 — Merchant Code
  3. 3Enter Merchant Code: RAYLANE EXPRESS
  4. 4Enter the exact trip amount
  5. 5Enter your FULL NAME as the reference
  6. 6Enter your PIN — you will receive an SMS
⚠ Reference name MUST match your booking name exactly or your ticket may be delayed.
A

Airtel Money

Pay using Airtel Money merchant service

  1. 1Dial *185*9# on your phone
  2. 2Select Pay Goods / Services
  3. 3Enter the Merchant Code
  4. 4Enter the exact trip amount
  5. 5Enter your FULL NAME as the reference
  6. 6Enter your PIN — you will receive an SMS
⚠ Payments with incorrect reference names may be delayed or rejected.

Scan & Pay Instantly

Use our QR code during booking checkout. Your MTN or Airtel Money app pre-fills the merchant, amount, and name reference — no manual entry, no errors. Available on the payment screen during booking.
